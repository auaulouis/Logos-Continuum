**[Saudi Arabia wants oil to stay between \$70 and \$80]{.underline}** a

President Donald Trump put public pressure on Riyadh to keep crude
prices in check, wanting to stop U.S. fuel costs rising ahead of the

shelved, **[Saudi Arabia]{.underline}** still **[wants to keep oil
